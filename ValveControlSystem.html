Spike-and-Hold Solenoid Valve Control System

Desired Spike and Hold Signal

The Completed Version of Control System

A control system is designed and deployed to independently control the opening of 8 solenoid valves. Special considerations has been taken to implement the control signal to be spike-and-hold, as required by the valve datasheet.

I used the differential amplifier to generate the signal. Specifically, I subtracted a charging capacitor's voltage from a steady 12V signal. In addition, I implemented a user-friendly design where via a 3 way toggle, the valve switch into 1. manual off 2. automatic control by software 3. manual on, with LEDs indicating the states of each valve.

Together with designing the PCB board, I also wrote Python scripts that controls an NI-DAQ device and send digital outputs to automatically control the valves, when the toggles on the PCB are switched to the auto mode.
